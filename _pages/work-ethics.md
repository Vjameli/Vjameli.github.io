How I got here is a long story, but what you need to know is that **I want to become a better mathematician!**

Ironically, it is not trivial to find unobstructed time to do math consistently while doing a PhD in Applied Mathematics.
It's easy to spend an entire month coding and realize you haven't touched a pencil even once.

So to ensure that I am both putting in the necessary hours and protecting time specifically for doing math, I have decided to track my hours and share them here for the world to see!

<u>Nothing like the good old social pressure</u> : )

I am using [Toggl](https://toggl.com/) to track my progress and will be attaching my monthly reports below. In the near future, I also plan to update this page with the specific materials I am studying outside of research.

If you have ideas on how I can improve this page, or simply want to send a few words of encouragement, please don't hesitate to reach out to me at **vinicius [dot] jameli [at] mail [dot] utoronto [dot] ca**.
